Our Company

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Our Service

We curate an extensive collection of plants, ranging from indoor houseplants to outdoor garden varieties. Our team is knowledgeable about different plant species and can assist customers in selecting the right plants for their specific needs, whether it's a low-maintenance succulent or a vibrant flowering plant.

Our Achievement

Our commitment to providing excellent customer service has resulted in a loyal customer base. We take pride in creating a warm and friendly atmosphere, ensuring that our customers have an enjoyable and memorable experience every time they visit.

Our News

We have recently expanded our collection to include a variety of air plants. These unique plants don't require soil to grow and can be displayed in creative ways, such as hanging terrariums or mounted on driftwood. Air plants are known for their low maintenance requirements and can add a touch of elegance to any space.
